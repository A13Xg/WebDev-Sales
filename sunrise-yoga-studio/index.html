Our Philosophy

Where stillness meets strength

At Sunrise Yoga Studio, we believe yoga is a practice — not a performance. Every class is an invitation to slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with what matters. Our small-group format means you're never just a number on a mat.

Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a lifelong practice, you'll find a welcoming space here. Founder Sarah Moreno built this studio on the principle that healing and growth thrive in community.

Our Story

Founded in stillness, built in community

Sunrise Yoga Studio opened its doors in 2016 when founder Sarah Moreno converted a sun-soaked warehouse loft into a space she wished had existed for her own healing journey. After a decade of teaching in large gyms and online platforms, she craved something smaller, warmer, and more intentional.

Every detail of the studio — from the hand-plastered walls to the organic cotton bolsters — was chosen with care. The schedule was built around the rhythms of real life: early risers, lunch-break practitioners, and evening unwinders all find their niche here.

Today Sunrise serves a community of over 200 regular practitioners, runs seasonal workshops, and remains, first and foremost, a place where you feel seen from the moment you walk through the door.

Sarah Moreno

Founder & Lead Instructor, E-RYT 500
