Our Story

From Seed to Cup — We Know Every Step

Artisan Coffee Roasters was founded on a simple belief: that extraordinary coffee begins long before the roast. We travel to origin, build direct relationships with the farmers who tend these crops, and pay well above fair-trade pricing — because quality at the source makes everything else possible.

Back in our roastery, our head roaster brings decades of experience to every batch. We roast in small lots to preserve the delicate flavors developed by altitude, soil, and careful harvesting — and we ship within 48 hours of roasting, so your cup captures the coffee at its peak.

Each coffee we carry tells the story of its place. We select coffees that are distinctive, traceable, and roasted to express — not mask — their inherent character.

Light Roast

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

Gedeo Zone, Ethiopia · 1,800–2,200m

Bright and floral with notes of jasmine, bergamot, and a lemon-honey finish. A naturally processed gem from one of coffee's birthplaces.

Medium Roast

Colombia El Paraíso

Huila, Colombia · 1,600–1,900m

Velvety body with caramel sweetness, red cherry, and a subtle dark chocolate undertone. Exceptionally balanced — ideal for pourover or French press.

Dark Roast

Sumatra Mandheling Reserve

North Sumatra, Indonesia · 1,200–1,500m

Earthy, full-bodied, and complex. Deep cedar, dark cocoa, and a smooth, low-acid finish that lingers. A roastery signature and perennial favorite.

Our Craft

How We Roast

Every batch we roast follows a meticulous process developed over years of direct-trade relationships and hands-on experimentation.

01

Source

We visit origin farms, cup extensively, and buy only what earns a place in our lineup. Direct-trade relationships give us full transparency.

02

Profile

Each new green coffee is sampled and profiled before a single production batch is roasted. We develop a custom roast curve for every origin.

03

Roast

Small-batch drum roasting by hand. We monitor temperature curves in real time, listening and adjusting to coax the best from each lot.

04

Ship

Orders ship within 48 hours of roasting — no sitting in a warehouse. Your coffee arrives at peak freshness, ready to brew immediately.
