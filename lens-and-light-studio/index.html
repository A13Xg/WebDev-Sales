About the Studio

Ten Years of Chasing the Perfect Light

Lens & Light Photography Studio was founded on one conviction: that a photograph should make you feel something. Not just document a moment, but hold it — give it texture, atmosphere, and the ability to transport you back.

Our team brings a decade of experience shooting weddings, portraits, and brand campaigns across the Pacific Northwest. We've built a studio space designed for natural light, and we carry that same philosophy into every location shoot: we don't overpower a scene, we learn to read it.

— The Lens & Light Team

How It Works

A Session from Inquiry to Delivery

01

Inquiry

Fill out the booking form with your session type, preferred dates, and a bit about what you're envisioning. We respond within 24 hours.

02

Consultation

A short call or exchange to understand your goals, walk through the session plan, select a location, and confirm the booking.

03

The Shoot

We come prepared, we direct with a light touch, and we make sure you're comfortable. The best images come from people who are at ease.

04

Delivery

Fully retouched, high-resolution images delivered via private online gallery within 2–3 weeks. Print-ready files included with every package.
