04 — Process

How I Work With Clients

Straightforward from first conversation to final delivery. No vague timelines, no surprise invoices.

01

Discovery Call

We spend 30–45 minutes going through your project goals, technical constraints, and timeline. I ask a lot of questions — understanding context is where good solutions start.

02

Scope & Proposal

I write a clear project scope document with deliverables, milestones, and a fixed or time-based fee structure depending on the project type. No ambiguous "and other duties as assigned."

03

Development & Collaboration

I work in short cycles and share progress regularly. You'll never go more than a week without seeing where things stand — and I'm always reachable during business hours.

04

Delivery & Handoff

Shipping isn't the end — it's the beginning. I document everything, walk your team through the codebase, and remain available for questions after handoff. Ongoing retainers available for continued support.
