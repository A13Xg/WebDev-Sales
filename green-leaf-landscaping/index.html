Our Story

Rooted in the Community We Serve

Green Leaf Landscaping started with a single truck and a belief that every property deserves to look its best. Over fifteen years later, we're proud to be the landscaping crew that neighbors trust and refer to their friends — season after season.

We're a local, family-run operation. That means when you call us, you get a real answer from someone who actually cares about your yard. No call centers, no subcontractors you've never met. Just our team, showing up on time and doing the job right.

Honest pricing — clear quotes before we start, no surprise charges when we're done
Reliable scheduling — we show up on the day we say we will, rain or shine
Licensed & insured — full liability coverage on every job, residential and commercial
